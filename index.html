Our Farm

BlueSkye farm is a small family farm in Wisconsin’s Driftless area, in the beautiful Town of Vermont situated between Mount Horeb and Black Earth Wisconsin just outside of Madison. We are small scale produces of forage based lamb from our flock of Katahdin hair sheep. Katahdin’s are a hardy, low maintenance breed of sheep that shed their coarse, dense winter coat each spring. This reduces the costs for produces focused on high quality meat production while reducing handling of the sheep due to shearing, and eliminates the need for tail docking. The Katahdin’s produce a medium size carcass with a mild flavor and naturally lean, tender meat. We offer lambs for sale and occasionally breeding stock as well, contact us for more information. Thank you.
